INFORMED CONSENT FORM
Urdu Speech Recognition Research

Greetings!

We are students from the National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (FAST-NUCES) conducting research on Urdu speech recognition systems.

PURPOSE OF THE RESEARCH:
We are developing a Speech-to-Text (STT) system that can understand conversations in the Urdu language, specifically for the real estate domain. This system will help automate customer service for property inquiries in Pakistan.

YOUR PARTICIPATION:
- We will record your voice as you speak some sentences in Urdu
- The recording will not exceed 2-3 minutes
- You can stop the recording at any time without any consequences
- Your participation is completely voluntary

DATA PRIVACY & CONFIDENTIALITY:
- Your voice recordings will be used solely for research purposes
- Your personal information (name, address, phone number) will be kept secure and confidential
- Your name will not be used in the research publication
- Data will be stored on secure servers with restricted access
- Audio recordings may be transcribed but will not be shared publicly
- You have the right to withdraw your data at any time

RISKS & BENEFITS:
- There are no known risks associated with this research
- You will not receive direct compensation
- Your contribution will help advance Urdu language technology

USE OF DATA:
The collected audio will be used to:
- Train and test automatic speech recognition models
- Evaluate system performance (Word Error Rate, accuracy)
- Improve understanding of Urdu phonetics and dialects
