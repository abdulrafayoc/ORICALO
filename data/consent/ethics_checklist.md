### Cultural & Linguistic Considerations

- [ ] **Language Accessibility**
  - Consent forms in participant's preferred language
  - Interpreter available if needed
  
- [ ] **Cultural Sensitivity**
  - Research team trained on cultural norms
  - Respect for local customs maintained

---

### Compliance Documentation

- [ ] **Records Maintained**
  - Signed consent forms filed
  - Ethics approval documents accessible
  - Data handling logs updated
  
- [ ] **Audit Trail**
  - Data collection log maintained
  - Participant tracking anonymized
  - Regular compliance reviews scheduled

### Special Considerations for Voice Data

- [ ] **Voice Biometrics**
  - Participants informed voice is biometric data
  - Voice data cannot uniquely identify individuals after anonymization
  
- [ ] **Synthetic Speech Generation**
  - If using voice for TTS, explicit consent obtained
  - Voice synthesis limitations explained
