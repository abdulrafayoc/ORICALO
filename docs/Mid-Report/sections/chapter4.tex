\section{RAG Corpus Schema}

The processed property corpus uses a unified JSONL format optimized for semantic retrieval. Table~\ref{tab:corpus-schema} shows the complete schema.

\begin{table}[!htbp]
\centering
\caption{RAG Corpus Schema}
\label{tab:corpus-schema}
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.2}
\begin{tabular}{|l|l|p{6cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Field} & \textbf{Type} & \textbf{Description} \\ \hline
\texttt{Property\_Id} & string & Unique identifier \\ \hline
\texttt{City} & string & City name (Lahore, Islamabad, Karachi) \\ \hline
\texttt{Location} & string & Locality/sector name \\ \hline
\texttt{Long Location} & string & Full address \\ \hline
\texttt{Property Type} & string & House, Plot, Flat, Commercial \\ \hline
\texttt{Price} & float & Listed price in PKR \\ \hline
\texttt{Price in words} & string & Localized price (``3 Crore 50 Lakh'') \\ \hline
\texttt{Bedrooms} & int & Number of bedrooms \\ \hline
\texttt{Baths} & int & Number of bathrooms \\ \hline
\texttt{Area (Marla)} & float & Area in Marla units \\ \hline
\texttt{Short Desc} & string & Title/headline \\ \hline
\texttt{Long Desc} & string & Full description (for embeddings) \\ \hline
\texttt{Link} & string & Source URL \\ \hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\section{Data Pipeline Flow}

The data pipeline processes information from multiple sources:

\textbf{Data Sources:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Kaggle: Pakistan Property Landscape
    \item Kaggle: Lahore House Listings 2025
    \item OpenDataBay: Urban Real Estate
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Processing Pipeline:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Load CSVs}: Import raw data from sources
    \item \textbf{Schema Unification}: Map diverse column names to unified schema
    \item \textbf{Data Cleaning}: Handle missing values, normalize formats
    \item \textbf{JSONL Export}: Output to \texttt{rag\_corpus.jsonl}
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Indexing Pipeline:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Parse JSONL}: Read processed corpus
    \item \textbf{Generate Embeddings}: Use SentenceTransformer model
    \item \textbf{ChromaDB Insert}: Store documents with metadata
\end{enumerate}
